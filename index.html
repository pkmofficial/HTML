semantic elements can be referred from w3 or mdn. elements used for different purposes - like header , nav , div , section , article , aside , footer
every element can be of 2 types based on its default display value - inline(takes minimum space) or block(occupies max space from left to right of screen - may also have margin,padding by default)

example of inline : a , b , i , sup , sub , abbr , img , span , textarea , input , code , select , br etc

example of block : div , p , header , body , footer , aside , h , hr , section , video , article , nav , form , table , pre , ol , ul , li etc
